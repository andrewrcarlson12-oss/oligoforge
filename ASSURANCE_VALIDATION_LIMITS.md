# OligoForge Assurance validation limits

- AssaySBOM is a molecular and computational bill of materials, not proof of assay performance.
- Sequence snapshots describe supplied files. They do not establish population prevalence, sampling independence, geographic representativeness or database completeness.
- DriftGuard reports model-based product and probe-recognition changes. It cannot determine efficiency, fluorescence, inhibition, matrix effects, LOD/LOQ, analytical sensitivity, clinical sensitivity/specificity or regulatory acceptability.
- Polymerase-specific mismatch tolerance is not inferred without experimental evidence.
- OFVR is an OligoForge-local record type, not an external vulnerability standard.
- A stable result means stable within the supplied snapshots and declared model, not guaranteed future stability.
- Aegis multi-edit search, FutureProof design and automated Repair Compiler are not implemented in this release.
- Expert review and laboratory bridging validation remain required before changing an operational assay.
